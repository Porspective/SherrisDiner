Our Story

Since 1988, Sherri's Diner has been the heartbeat of South Oklahoma City. We aren't just a restaurant; we're a time machine. From the jukebox spinning golden oldies to the smell of fresh coffee at 6 AM, we preserve the classic American diner experience.

Our commitment to "Real Food for Real People" means everything is made from scratch. We hand-bread our chicken fried steaks daily and peel our own potatoes. No shortcuts, just good food.
